About Me

I was born in Queretaro, Mexico and moved to the United States at the age of 9. I lived in Austin, TX for 20 years. I graduated from St. Edward's University in 2014 with a Dual degree in Accounting Information Technology. I got married in 2011 and have 2 beautiful daughters. My hobbies include running, reading, and eating. In the last month my husband and I started our own business venture.
